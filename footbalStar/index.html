Pope Okolo

Pope Uzonna Okolo is a 7ft1 17 year old forward at GSC F.C Surulere Lagos. At age 16 Pope announced himself to the world when he scored a spectacular hat trick in 20 minutes after he was subtituted in 70th minutes to go, the goals took his team to Semi-final of Governors cup. He became the youngest player to score hat trick and the fastest hat trick in the compitition.
